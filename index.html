What is famous here?

"Thakali Khana" is a traditional meal originating from the Thakali people of Nepal. It is renowned for its balanced and flavorful components, typically including rice, lentils, meat, vegetables, and a variety of pickles and condiments.

Keema Noodles

Keema Noodles is a delicious and flavorful fusion dish that combines the spiced ground meat (keema) with noodles. It's a popular street food and home-cooked meal in many parts of South Asia.

Rice Krispie Treats

To make Rice Krispie Treats, melt butter in a large pan and then mix in mini marshmallows until fully melted and smooth. Stir in Rice Krispies cereal until evenly coated with the marshmallow mixture. Press the mixture into a greased pan, and let it cool and set. Once firm, cut into squares and enjoy these simple, crispy, and sweet treats.
